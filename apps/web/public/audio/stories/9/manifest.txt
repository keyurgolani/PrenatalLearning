The workers string these amino acids together like beads on a necklace, and when they're done, they've made a protein!! Proteins are the workers of your body. They build your muscles, carry oxygen in your blood, fight off germs, and do thousands of other jobs. Every protein started as a recipe written in your DNA, translated through this beautiful dance of molecules.! The Dance of Replication!
