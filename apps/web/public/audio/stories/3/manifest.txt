This brings us to one of the strangest ideas in all of science: superposition.! Imagine, little one, that you have a magical coin. A scientist named Erwin Schrödinger wanted to show just how strange this idea is. He imagined a thought experiment; not a real experiment, but a story to make us think.! Imagine a cat in a sealed box, little one. Inside the box is a tiny bit of radioactive material that might or might not release a particle in the next hour. If it does, a mechanism breaks a vial of something that would put the cat to sleep forever. If it doesn't, the cat stays perfectly fine.! Now, according to quantum mechanics, until we open the box and look, the radioactive particle is in superposition; it both has and hasn't released. And if that's true, then the cat is also in superposition; both asleep forever AND perfectly fine, at the same time!! Of course, this seems absurd.
